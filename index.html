Having departed from his homeland, Kratos starts a new life with his son, Atreus, in the Norse world.

Kratos and Atreus embark on a journey to discover answers before the prophesied apocalypse, Ragnarök, ensues.

Gameplay Overview

WEAPONS

With monsters and cruel gods across the realms, carrying a robust weapon is mandatory. Should enhancements be needed, the dwarves have a range of upgrade collections for an affordable price. Each upgrade or runic provides an upperhand against feeble or tough enemies.

REALMS

The nine realms, entwined to a world tree, are a part of the Norse grand cosmic. Each boasts a distinct feature, with some having occupants who thrive amongst the great gods. However, some are uninhabited and hostile to travelers.

CHARACTERS

Characters are cruical keys for the adventure. Some play a major part and affect the flow of the story. The rest may not but have side quests that reward astonishing items as a form of gratitude.

CHESTS

Chests are little tokens from the nature for overcoming hardships and riddles. However, deep within tests the luck and the conviction of a traveler. Mediocre to lavish, all items serve their own purpose, one that cannot be understimated.
