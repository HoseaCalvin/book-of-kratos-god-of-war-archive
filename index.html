Having departed from his homeland, Kratos starts a new life with his son, Atreus, in the Norse world.

Kratos and Atreus embark on a journey to unearth truths before the prophesied apocalypse, Ragnarök, ensues.

Gameplay Overview

WEAPONS

In the ruthless Norse world, every warrior must possess the right tools to thrive. Wield mythical weapons and command the nature to your will. Along the journey, meet knowledgeable dwarves who might barter their expertise for hacksilver.

THE NINE REALMS

The Nine Realms, each entwined to Yggdrasil, are part of the Norse tapestry. Traverse the luminous and the murky realms to discover exotic relics and its alluring lore.

CHARACTERS

Meet prominent characters along your adventure in the diverse Norse world. Not everyone has equal benevolence, some may conceal their true intention to deceive or harm.

CHESTS

Chests can be discovered whether in plain sight or deep within caves. Indulge your heart and treat every reward as a complement to your journey, regardless of its rarity.
