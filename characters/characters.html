CHARACTERS

A Helping Hand

Along the path are benevolent characters who are the key to the journey. Some of them might have a favor, with exquisite items as a return for the trouble. On the other hand, others are willing to sell or buy items for an affordable price.

Treachery or Death

Violent figures know not about remorse or even feelings. Whether provoked or not, their bloodthirst will spiral out and drive to kill. A number of them are well-equipped and powerful, but weak to certain elements.
