Muspelheim

Realm of Fire

Fire and Brimstone

The molten grounds of Muspelheim only greet the formidable with a strong desire to explore.
Its hills gnaw the burning sky, spitting lava that scorches skin in the matter of seconds. Also, the thick smoke obscures eyesight, making the journey arduous with fiery monsters around. Across the realms lie the legacy of fire giants and their mighty ruler, Surtr.

Major Regions

The Trial Chambers

The three chambers, aligned next to one another, mark the bygone legacy of fire giants. At the center of each stands the legendary sword of Surtr's, a symbol Asgard's inevitable catastrophe. Inscribed upon the hilt are runics describing a collection of trials, demanding equal prowess and valor. In the end, those who thrive are bestowed grandeur gifts of Surtr, a blessing for greater glory.

The Crucible

Another Surtr's tests for the mightiest, The Crucible summons ruthless, unyielding monsters to ravage those who dare to try.
Its circular arena stands before the mountain, with each diverging pathway leading to an enclosed, smaller arena. Upon the hilt of his sword lie the inscriptions of the challenges, each requiring better wits and strikes to count. The triumphant is blessed with Surtr's gifts, as a pledge for longevity and force.

Burning Cliffs

A river of scorching lava from the mountain's breath, Burning Cliffs invites the brazen who dare to stroll through an inferno. Up ahead are winding passages, ancient caverns, and steep ascents waiting to sing a song about those who thrive upon besting the nature's wrath.

Molten Lowlands

The foothill of the fuming mountain, Molten Lowlands overlooks the blazing horizon of Muspelheim. Lava rivers and fire giant monuments, the sightings even the dead will never praise. Those who are in dire need of a breath may lie at ease, although the fire never pauses to usurp your energy.

Surtr's Forge

The workshop and shelter of Muspelheim's long-standing king, Surtr's Forge stands as a monument of forthcoming apocalypse. Reverberating clinks of a hammer awake the monsters, marking Surtr's relentless effort to forge a fiery sword. Despite his race, he miserably fails in forging one, thus many brittle swords are left in ruins.
