Helheim

Realm of the Dead

Unending Curse

Anyone deemed dishonorable by the Valkyries are met with the blistering cold of Helheim, a realm where no mortals return from.
Their souls are watched upon, kept tightly in the grasp of Hræsvelgr, until time knows not when to stop. However, whosoever brought shame and destruction faces a graver fate. Their spirits journey to a shore where a hungry serpent awaits for supper.

Major Regions

Bridge of the Damned

Known as the point of no return, the Bridge of the Damned stands the final gate for the dishonorable. Watched upon by the bridge keeper, it ensures the souls remain dead without the interruption of the living. Should they have the temerity to approach, the keeper calls for a duel in the unyielding blizzard.

Docks of Vaðgelmir

Overlooking the soundless horizon, The Docks of Vaðgelmir are the main harbor of Helheim.
Its piers harbor floating empty vessels, waiting to transport the chosen to war in Ragnarök. However, for a thousand years long, the souls have departed from Helheim.

The Silent Clearing

A prison of the Hel Hound, Garm, The Silent Clearing is not welcome for even the witful gods.
The hound slumbers deep in loneliness, its feet shackled tightly as a means to preserve peace in the Nine Realms. Were the beast set loose, it would slice the thin air to open rifts and gnaw on the innocents.
