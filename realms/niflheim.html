Niflheim

Realm of the Mist

A Soulless Realm

Niflheim, one of the two primordial realms, features a soulless atmosphere and thick mist that obscures vision.
The desolate fields scream a call to run, instilling a sense of hazard to travellers who wander around the realm. Many met their demise, but some never received the afterlife, instead resurrected as monsters to prey on those who knew not the bounds of the Norse cosmos.

Major Regions

The Labyrinth

The infamous labyrinth of Niflheim, once explored by the Ivaldi brothers, awaits those who dare to test their mettle! Veiled in thick and toxic fog, it teems with abominable monsters and concealed deadly traps. To retrieve the treasures, it demands not only valor but also brute strength!

The Raven Tree

The mystical ravens, once under Odin's manipulation to watch the realms, now perch atop the twigs of a tree. There, they mourn together, expressing their bygone struggles to unbind themselves from his restraints. At the very least, no vile gods will ever abuse them again.

Mist Fields

Mist Fields, desolate and uninviting to any travellers, are shrouded in thick mist that invites uncertainty. They display a variety of terrains, ranging from deep caverns to towering hills, all frozen and glittering. Alongside the pathways lie the trails of their predecessors, the humans and gods who now settle solemnly across the Nine Realms.
