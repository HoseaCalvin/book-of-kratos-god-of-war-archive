Major Regions

The Labyrinth

Veiled in toxic mist, The Labyrinth guards enigmatic artifacts originating from every realm. A lair of ferocious behemoths, this expanse only welcomes seasoned warriors with unyielding grit.

The Raven Tree

The Raven Tree, isolated beneath Niflheim's frigid atmosphere, endures as their final resting place. Once under Odin's rigid authority, these spectral ravens echo their endless torment.

Mist Fields

Blanketed with icy mist, Mist Fields endures as a buried legacy of the frost Jötnar. Their scattered remnants bear silent witness to their inevitable extinction.
