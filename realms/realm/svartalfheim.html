Svartalfheim

Realm of the Dark Elves

Land of the Blacksmiths

Major Regions

Aurvangar Wetlands

Stretching far to the heart of Svartalfheim, the primary conduit to navigate through the dwarven realm is Aurvangar Wetlands.
Alongside the waterways are the splendor of dwarven architectural marvels, constructed atop the arid and elevated terrains. Abandoned long ago by the craftsmanship masters, the architectures are no longer occupied.

Niðavellir

The stronghold of the dwarves, Niðavellir, is the heart of the dwarven realm.
The area features elevated terrains, thus, some lofts are seamlessly interwoven with them, allowing fortified shelter and mine construction. Lastly, advanced mechanisms are also developed to accommodate their needs or streamline work processes.

Bay of Bounty

The vast, clear bay sitting adjacent to the dwarven settlement enhances aesthetics of Svartalfheim wonders.
Named Bay of Bounty, the bay connects to isolated islands, beaches, passages, and deep caverns. However, the dwarves possess the intelligence to outdo themselves. As a result, several oil rigs, mines, settlements, and other structures can be seen from distance.

Jarnsmida Pitmines

Jarnsmida Pitmines serve as a testament to dwarven brilliance of procuring scarce resources. Ingenious contraptions, efficient transportation methods, and other structures for their work. In addition, the quarry connects to several underground mines, granting more space to unearth underground resources.

The Applecore

Connected to Jarnsmida Pitmines, The Applecore is a stretched underground mine built as an extension for their industries. Like the pitmines, cutting-edge technologies are invented to expedite resources gathering. In addition, places for forging are strategically positioned to craft the necessary tools for mining.

The Forge

Atop the mountain exists a glittering swamp beneath which the dwarves believe to be The Forge.
Far from the mundane forge in human interpretations, but rather, a sacred place weapons enchanted with arcane power. Deep underwater lies a mysterious being whose presence is indispensable to the forging process. However, an offering must be handed in advance.
