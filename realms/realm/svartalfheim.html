Svartalfheim

Realm of the Dwarves

Land of the Blacksmiths

Major Regions

Aurvangar Wetlands

Aurvangar Wetlands, a primary conduit into the heart of Svartalfheim, acts as a crucial gateway. The dwarves, leveraging the surging geysers, engineered advanced checkpoints, accessible to those with profound knowledge. Though no dwarves remain in the territory, the abandoned structures attest to their unparalleled brilliance.

Niðavellir

The dwarven domain, Niðavellir, embodies their exceptional craftsmanship. Harnessing sophisticated mechanisms and natural resources, this industrial nexus elevates their crafts to an unmatched caliber. Mjölnir and the Leviathan Axe, forged by Brok and Sindri, stand as their pinnacle achievements known across realms.

Bay of Bounty

The Bay of Bounty, cloaked in humid highlands, mirrors the enigmatic nature of the dwarven realm. Its vast waters, with dispersed islands and rich marine life, make every pilgrimage an enthralling story worth recounting.

Jarnsmida Pitmines

Jarnsmida Pitmines, a reflection of dwarven ingenuity and versatility, is a crowning monolith in their industrial legacy. Creaking cranes and shifting rails breathe life into the colossal quarry, transporting scarce ores from the heart of Svartalfheim.

The Applecore

The Applcore, notorious for its peril and fractured ground, plunges into the bedrock of Svartalfheim. Harboring primordial materials, it sustains the delicate forging tradition and anchors the balance of the Nine Realms.

The Forge

The Forge, shrouded deep within a mountain, remains a mythical relic in the Norse tapestry. A mermaid of unknown origin blesses offerings which the dwarves deem divine. She serves only the worthy who possess all soul fragments, endowing her ethereal favor.
