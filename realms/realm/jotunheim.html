Jötunheim

Realm of the Jötnar

A Dreary Wasteland

Major Regions

The Tallest Peak

Regarded as the highest point in all of Nine Realms, this unnamed peak was an altar of their kind. Its purpose remains unknown to the outside world, brought far away by the giants who sought shelter elsewhere or lost their soul. As of now, this forsaken place stands as a commemoration of those who settled to Midgard.

Ironwood

The forest of the Jötnar, known as Ironwood, is a haven of the Jötnar refugees and a major habitat of the wildlife. Those who survived the Aesir attacks are thought to have been entrenched in the woods, consequently driving Odin to confirm the rumor. Continuous failed attempts have caused Odin to abandon his quest, leaving the region an unsolved mystery.

Vimur River

Situated adjacent to a treehouse, Vimur River boasts a winding waterway, tall rocks, and humungous trees overlooking the river. To set foot on the Highest Peak valley, this river remains the primary passage to venture safely.

The Glittering Fields

Here lie the Jötnar who died honorably during the Aesir genocide, who proudly preserved the realm but paid the ultimate price. The corpses echoing their sorrows, flooding the fields with blood and tears, etching a dreadful mark on the realm. Though their souls are absent, they will forever be the protectors of Jötunheim until Ragnarök storms the universe.
