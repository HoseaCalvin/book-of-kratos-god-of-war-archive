Jötunheim

Realm of the Jötnar

Trails of Primordial Force

Major Regions

The Giant's Fingers

The highest peak across the Nine Realms, called The Giant's Fingers, stands firmly to witness the Jötnar's endurance amidst the devastation wrought by the Aesir. At its zenith rests an altar to commemorate the Jötnar who settled in Midgard, now fallen or vanished into a distant refuge, far beyond mortal sight.

Ironwood

The forest of Jötunheim, Ironwood, is the last refuge of the remaining Jötnar. Its looming trees and thick vegetation veil their legacy and archaic shrine of prophecy. Once under the sight of the Allfather, it now endures as a haven of their spirits from today and beyond.

Vimur River

Vimur River, with its sparkling reddish-brown water and sharp banks, serves as a prominent route through Jötunheim. The indistinguished flora and fauna in view are native to the realm, a hallmark of exquisite wildlife unparalleled across the realms.

The Glittering Fields

The Glittering Fields, shrouded in illusion woven by Jötnar, stand as Jötunheim's most enchanting wonders. To those outside the Jötnar's domain, the fields may reveal fallen Jötnar strewn across jagged plains. Legend holds that even Heimdall, the god of foresight, cannot pierce through the illusion, a testament to the Jötnar's sorcery.
