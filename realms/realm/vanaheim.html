Vanaheim

Realm of the Vanir

The Nature's Gift

Major Regions

The Southern Wilds

A majestic forest in the southern reaches, it serves as a bastion of the indigenous wildlife. Its lush vegetation, abounding with rich resources and towering in size, cloaks the fauna within from lurking predators.

Freyr's Camp

Amid the Aesir-Vanir conflict, the surviving Vanir were compelled to seek both provisions and retreat. Consequently, Freyr and his loyal companions entrenched themselves deep beneath a colossal, sprawling tree. In the fleeting moments of peace, they would commemorate their fallen kin.

Eastern Barri Woods

With its mountain ranges overlooking the verdant plains, Eastern Barri Woods offers an inexplicable glamor for passing voyagers. The Jötnar foretell that the celestial wolves, Skӧll and Hati, will pursue the Sun and Moon respectively, until Ragnarök ensues.

Western Barri Woods

Western Barri Woods, identical to its eastern counterpart, boasts an enthralling scene exclusive to Vanaheim. Before mortal eyes stands stretching mountain ridges, great landmass bordering an arid canyon, a witness to Vanaheim's unparalleled fertility.

The Northern Wilds

The Northern Wilds, under the occupation of the Aesir, is home to historical Vanir sanctuaries. Exquisite statues and sacred rite are a mark of the Vanir tradition, preserved for thousands of winters, now tarnished by the Aesir. A fleeting legacy, while remembered by few, no longer holds weight to the present civilization.

The Crater

The Crater, scarred by an unknown catalyst, embodies true atrocities of the Aesir. Despite its stark desolation, this once flourished with abundant resources and diverse wildlife. It is widely attested that Odin orchestrated the drought into the region, a classic endeavor to assert dominance over the Vanir.
