Major Regions

Bridge of The Damned

The departed complete their pilgrimage by crossing The Bridge of The Damned, a cursed threshold into Helheim. Relentlessly sensing the passing souls, its sentinel ensures the living do not trespass the dominion of the dead.

Docks of Vaðgelmir

The Docks of Vaðgelmir, sitting in the central reaches of the realm, harbor the ghostly vessels of Helheim. When Ragnarök ensues, they ferry the damned warriors through the mystical barrier of Asgard.

The Silent Clearing

Beyond mortal reaches, The Silent Clearing is the eternal prison of the Hel Hound. Its tight shackles restrain the soulless beast to maintain the Norse cosmic order.
