Asgard

Realm of the Aesir

The Seat of Cosmic Order

Major Regions

Plains of Ida

Plains of Ida, sometimes acknowledged as Iðavöllr, serve as the sacred grounds of the Aesir stronghold. Engulfed by ephemeral mountain ranges, its vast landscape boasts verdant marshland, rugged terrains, and glistening water.

Hrimthur's Wall

Shielding the stronghold of the Aesir, Hrimthur's Wall is the ultimate stave against the untamed force of nature. The legends tell of a cunning Jötnar, Hrimthur, who constructed the wall as part of a murky deal with Odin. Unbeknownst to the Allfather himself, a structural flaw was concealed to help Surtr annihilate Asgard during Ragnarök.

Glaðsheim

The heart of Asgard, Glaðsheim, is home to the revered deities of the Aesir and the valiant Einherjar. With its legacy spanning thousands of winters, Glaðsheim stands as a resplendent fortress worthy of those hallowed by the Valkyries.
