Muspelheim

Realm of Fire

Fire and Brimstone

Major Regions

The Trial Chambers

Demanding immense strength and cunning, The Trial Chambers stand as a testament to Surtr's unforgiving challenges. Those who triumph shall be endowed with items of immeasurable worth.

The Crucible

The Crucible, another Surtr's chamber of trials, hallows the unrivaled brawler in the Norse realm. Every heinous beast, each possessing unfathomable abilities, demands various tactics to overcome.

Burning Cliffs

Overlooking the realm's scorching wonder, Burning Cliffs highlight the tumultuous nature of Muspelheim. The streams of molten lava, burning pits, and steep ascents transform any pilgrimage into a test of endurance.

Molten Lowlands

Molten Lowlands, nestled at the mountain's foot, remain a secluded expanse beneath the fuming haze. Bathed in Muspelheim's furious breath, the very air incinerates even the thickest skin.

Surtr's Forge

The workshop of Muspelheim's long-standing king, Surtr's Forge looms as a monolith of the inevitable apocalypse, Ragnarök. It is rumored where the catastrophe-incarnate forges his flaming sword, a mirror to his undying endeavor.
